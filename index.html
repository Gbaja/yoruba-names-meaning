    Yorùbá

    Yoruba is one of the three main ethnic groups in Nigeria. Yoruba states are located in southwestern Nigeria. Yoruba people are native speakers of the Yoruba language.

    Names are important to Yoruba people, and it is generally believed that people live their life out based on their name. As a result, Yoruba put lots of thought into coming up with a name, including having naming ceremonies 7 days after the birth of a child.
